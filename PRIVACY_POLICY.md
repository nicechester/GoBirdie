# Privacy Policy for GoBirdie

**Last Updated: April 20, 2026**

## Overview

GoBirdie ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our iOS application.

## 1. Information We Collect

### Location Data
- **GPS Coordinates**: We collect your precise GPS location to track golf shots and calculate distances during rounds
- **Purpose**: To display your position on course maps and calculate shot distances
- **Storage**: Location data is stored locally on your device and only sent to your Apple Watch when syncing

### Health and Fitness Data
- **Heart Rate**: If you enable HealthKit integration, we may read heart rate data during rounds
- **Purpose**: To provide optional fitness tracking during golf
- **Storage**: Heart rate data is stored with your round records

### Round Data
- **Score Information**: Hole scores, putts, clubs used, and shot locations
- **Course Information**: Course name, hole par, and yardage data
- **Purpose**: To track your golf performance and statistics
- **Storage**: All round data is stored locally on your device

### Device Information
- **Device Type and OS Version**: To ensure app compatibility
- **App Usage**: Crash reports and error logs to improve app stability
- **Purpose**: App performance optimization and bug fixes

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:
- Track and display your golf rounds and statistics
- Calculate distances and provide course information
- Sync data between iPhone and Apple Watch
- Sync rounds to desktop companion app (optional)
- Improve app performance and fix bugs
- Provide location-based course discovery

## 3. Data Storage and Security

- **Local Storage**: Most data is stored locally on your device using encrypted storage
- **iCloud Sync**: If enabled, round data may sync to your iCloud account
- **Desktop Sync**: Rounds can optionally be synced to GoBirdie Desktop app via local network
- **No Cloud Servers**: We do not maintain centralized servers storing your golf data
- **No Third-Party Sharing**: Your golf data is never shared with third parties for marketing or analytics

## 4. Third-Party Services

### OpenStreetMap
- Used for course mapping and exploration
- Visit their privacy policy: https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Privacy_Policy

### Apple Services
- **Location Services**: Apple's location framework
- **HealthKit**: If enabled, integrated with Apple's HealthKit framework
- **WatchConnectivity**: Data synced between iPhone and Apple Watch
- **iCloud**: Optional backup of round data

## 5. Permissions We Request

### Required Permissions
- **Location Services**: GPS access for shot tracking and course mapping
- **Motion & Fitness**: Activity data for workout sessions

### Optional Permissions
- **HealthKit**: Heart rate and fitness data (if you enable it)
- **File Access**: For importing/exporting round data

## 6. Data Retention

- **On-Device Data**: Retained indefinitely on your device
- **Deleted Data**: When you delete a round, it is permanently removed from your device
- **iCloud Backups**: Follow Apple's iCloud backup retention policies

## 7. Children's Privacy

GoBirdie is not intended for users under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information.

## 8. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Delete your golf round data at any time
- Disable location services in iOS Settings
- Revoke HealthKit access in iOS Settings
- Opt out of iCloud sync

## 9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
